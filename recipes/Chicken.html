The Ultimate Chicken Breast

Description:

This simple and flavorful chicken breast recipe combines tender, juicy chicken with a delicious herb-infused marinade of olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, thyme, and Dijon mustard. The chicken is seasoned and allowed to marinate for a bit, ensuring every bite is packed with savory goodness. It’s then seared in a hot skillet until golden and crisp on the outside while remaining juicy on the inside. A splash of white wine or chicken broth is added to create a rich sauce that enhances the flavors, while a sprinkle of fresh parsley adds a burst of color. Serve it with roasted vegetables, quinoa, or a light salad for a quick and satisfying meal that’s both healthy and full of flavor.

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme (or fresh, if preferred)
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon butter (optional for extra flavor)
  • Fresh parsley for garnish (optional)

Steps:

  1. Season the chicken: Rub the chicken breasts with olive oil, then sprinkle with garlic powder, onion powder, thyme, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  2. Marinate: (Optional) Let the chicken marinate for 10-15 minutes for more flavor.
  3. Cook the chicken: Heat a skillet over medium-high heat. Add the chicken and cook for 6-7 minutes per side until golden brown and cooked through (internal temperature should reach 165°F/75°C).
  4. Optional butter: In the last minute, add a tablespoon of butter for extra richness and flavor.
  5. Garnish and serve: Squeeze fresh lemon juice over the chicken and sprinkle with fresh parsley. Serve with your favorite sides.
