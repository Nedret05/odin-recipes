The One Meatball

Description

These simple meatballs are tender, flavorful, and easy to make. Ground beef is mixed with breadcrumbs, garlic, parsley, and a touch of parmesan, then seasoned with salt and pepper. The mixture is rolled into small balls and baked until golden and cooked through. Serve them with pasta, on a sandwich, or with a side of marinara for a quick and satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb (450g) ground beef
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup milk (optional, for moisture)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven: Set to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Mix ingredients: In a large bowl, combine ground beef, breadcrumbs, Parmesan, egg, garlic, parsley, oregano, salt, pepper, and milk (if using). Mix until well combined.
  3. Shape the meatballs: Roll the mixture into small balls, about 1 inch in diameter.
  4. Bake: Place the meatballs on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper and bake for 20-25 minutes, until cooked through and golden brown.
  5. Serve: Enjoy with pasta, on a sandwich, or with marinara sauce.
