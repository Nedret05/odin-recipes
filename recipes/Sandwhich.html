The Capricious Sandwich

Description

This simple sandwich recipe combines fresh, flavorful ingredients for a satisfying meal. Soft, toasted bread is spread with a creamy layer of mayonnaise and mustard, then piled high with tender slices of deli meat, crisp lettuce, juicy tomato, and a slice of sharp cheddar cheese. A few pickles add a nice tangy crunch, while the sandwich is finished off with a sprinkle of salt and pepper. Perfect for a quick lunch or a picnic, this sandwich is both delicious and easy to customize with your favorite ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread (whole wheat, white, or your choice)
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 1 teaspoon mustard
  • 4-6 slices deli meat (turkey, ham, or chicken)
  • 1 slice cheddar cheese (or cheese of choice)
  • 2-3 slices tomato
  • 2-3 leaves lettuce
  • 2-3 leaves lettuce
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Toast the bread: Lightly toast the bread slices until golden brown.
  2. Spread condiments: Spread mayonnaise on one slice and mustard on the other.
  3. Layer the ingredients: Place the deli meat, cheese slice, tomato, and lettuce on one slice of bread.
  4. Add pickles(Optional): Layer pickles on top for extra crunch.
  5. Assemble and season: Sprinkle with salt and pepper, then top with the second slice of bread.
  6. Serve: Slice the sandwich in half and enjoy!
